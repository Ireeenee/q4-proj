2nd Quarter Project


Bloom and Blossom in the Philippines

The Filipinos are known to put a smile on your face with their hospitality, kindness, and cheerfulness. Apart from the locals, another reason for your smiles to bloom and blossom is its local floral farms. Even with challenges regarding the tropical climates, these farms thrive beautifully.

Northern Blossom Flower Farm

This blissful farm is located in Atok, Benguet. It showcases many flowers, such as snapdragons, larkspurs, rice flower, matthiola, cabbage rose, and more. Apart from the flowers, Mt. Pulag and Mt. Timbak are nicely seen. You can never have too much nature in this farm.

Sirao Flower Farm

Amsterdam, Netherlands is known for their beauitful gardens, and this farm owns the title "Mini Amsterdam". It is located in Brgy. Sirao, Cebu City. The star flower in this farm is called "Celosia". These flowers provide as an alternative to tulips and hyacinths, due to our tropical climate.

Alejandra's flower garden

This farm is also known as "Buwakan ni Alejandra", and is located in Balamban, Cebu. It is full of ups and downs, but don't worry because 1.) Each path features a different design, from "The Lorax" to "Rainbow Bridges", and 2.) There's a nice cafe and/or restaurant to rest at the end. It's worth while exercise.

Sunflower Maze

Getting lost was never so fun and beautiful here in Tayug, Pangasinan! This 8,000 flower labryinth is as large as 2,100 square meters. The maze width can fit up to 4 people side by side! Apart from the maze itself, the farm also consists of other colorful views with other flowers such as marigold, petunia, and torenia.
